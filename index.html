My Favorite Hobby

I began collecting vinyl records in 2023. Growing up, music always brought me joy and a sense of escape. I used to collect CDs, but in today's world, everything is now digital. I miss the excitement of getting a new album and seeing the cover art, song lists, and artist details. Nostalgia drew me to vinyl collecting. I grew up surrounded by the music of iconic artists, whose work evokes nostalgia and reflects their profound impact on the industry. I appreciate their fashion and the evolution of music over time. Vinyl offers a unique sound quality, and finding rare records is a joy. My collection continues to grow, with some records holding sentimental value. This hobby has deepened my music appreciation and connected me with fellow collectors.
